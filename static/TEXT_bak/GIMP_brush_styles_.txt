To view all the brush styles available in GIMP, you can follow these steps:

1. Open GIMP on your computer.
2. Click on the "Paintbrush" icon from the toolbox on the left-hand side of the screen.
3. In the options panel at the bottom of the screen, click on the brush preview icon (it looks like a brushstroke) to open the Brush panel.
4. In the Brush panel, click on the "Brushes" tab to see all the available brush styles.
5. You can scroll through the list of brushes to see all the available styles, or you can use the search bar to find specific types of brushes.

Note that some brush styles may not be installed by default in GIMP, but you can download and install additional brushes from various sources online.
